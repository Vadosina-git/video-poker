### Full Description

Learn and practice video poker strategy at your own pace with Video Poker Trainer.

Video Poker Trainer is a study tool for the classic five-card draw poker family. Pick from 10 paytable variants and explore how the math changes hand by hand. Run single-hand or multi-hand sessions to drill correct holds against any paytable.

PAYTABLE VARIANTS:
• Jacks or Better — The classic baseline for beginners
• Bonus Poker — Extra payouts for four-of-a-kind
• Double Bonus — Higher rewards for quad aces
• Double Double Bonus — Kicker bonuses on four-of-a-kind
• Triple Double Bonus — Maximum four-of-a-kind variance
• Deuces Wild — All twos play as wild cards
• Joker Poker — 53-card deck with a wild joker
• Deuces and Joker — Five wild cards in play
• Bonus Poker Deluxe — Simplified bonus structure
• Aces and Faces — Face-card four-of-a-kind bonuses

PRACTICE MODES:
• Single Hand — Focused study, one hand at a time
• Triple Play — 3 hands in parallel
• Five Play — 5 hands in parallel
• Ten Play — 10 hands in parallel
• Ultra VP — Multiplier mechanic for the next round
• Spin Poker — 3×5 grid with 20 paylines

FEATURES:
• Paytable indicator on every screen
• Game depth indicator — see how long your bankroll lasts
• Multiple bet levels and denominations
• Full session statistics
• 3 languages: English, Russian, Spanish

No real money. Practice only — virtual credits, no purchases convert to cash.

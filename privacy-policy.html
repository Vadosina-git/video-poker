Privacy Policy — Video Poker Trainer

Effective date: 7 May 2026 · Last updated: 7 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Poker Trainer (the "App", bundle ID com.khralz.videopoker) handles user information. The App is published by Ivan Al Zeidi, an individual developer based in the United Arab Emirates ("we", "us", "the developer").

Short version: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about you. There are no user accounts, no analytics events, no advertising identifiers, and no behavioral tracking. The only data that leaves your device is (1) a randomly generated, non-personal device identifier used solely to deliver remote game configuration, and (2) anonymous purchase information processed by our in-app purchase provider when you make a purchase.

1. What we DO NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any other personal contact information.
  • We do not create user accounts. The App is fully playable offline once installed.
  • We do not collect or use advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID, or similar).
  • We do not use tracking, analytics, attribution, or behavioral profiling SDKs.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, or files.
  • We do not show third-party advertisements.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing.

2. What we DO collect (limited and non-personal)

2.1 Local game data (stays on your device)

The App saves your in-game progress (virtual coins, settings, daily quest state, theme preference, language) to local device storage. This data never leaves your device. It is removed automatically when you uninstall the App.

2.2 Anonymous device identifier for remote configuration

On first launch, the App generates a random UUID (app_instance_id) and stores it locally. This identifier is sent only to Google Firebase Remote Config when the App fetches game configuration updates (e.g. balancing tweaks). It is not linked to your name, email, device, advertising ID, or any other personal attribute, and we do not use it to track you across sessions for any analytical or marketing purpose. It exists solely so Firebase can deliver consistent configuration to the same client.

2.3 In-app purchase data

The App offers optional in-app purchases of virtual currency (coins). Purchases are processed by:

  • Apple App Store (on iOS) — see Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing (on Android) — see Google Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat as our IAP backend, which receives anonymous transaction metadata (product ID, purchase timestamp, anonymous device id, app version) to validate purchases and prevent fraud — see RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

We never receive your credit card, bank account, or billing address — that information stays with Apple, Google, and your payment processor.

2.4 Local notifications

If you opt in to notifications (you will be asked first), the App schedules reminders that fire locally on your device. No push tokens are sent to a remote server. You can disable notifications at any time in the App settings or the system Settings app.

3. Third parties

The App integrates the following services. We share only the minimum data needed for each service to function:

  • Google Firebase Remote Config — receives the random app_instance_id and basic device platform info (iOS/Android, app version) to deliver remote configuration. (Firebase Privacy)
  • RevenueCat — receives anonymous transaction data when you make a purchase. (RevenueCat Privacy)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — handles all purchase processing and app delivery. (Apple · Google)

4. Children's privacy

The App simulates casino-style video poker using virtual currency. It is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App does not contain real-money gambling — no virtual currency or item earned in the App can be cashed out, redeemed, or exchanged for real money or goods of monetary value.

5. Your rights

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information and you have no account with us, there is no personal profile to access, correct, or delete. To remove all locally stored game data, uninstall the App. The random app_instance_id stored on Firebase will become orphaned and cease to receive any data.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (GDPR), the United Kingdom (UK GDPR), California (CCPA/CPRA), or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, you may contact us using the email below to inquire about, or request deletion of, any data we may hold related to your installation.

6. Data retention

The random app_instance_id is retained by Firebase per Google's standard retention policy. We do not retain any other server-side records about you. Local data on your device is retained until you uninstall the App.

7. Security

All network communication between the App and the services listed above is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS).

8. International data transfers

The App is operated from the United Arab Emirates. Third-party services (Firebase, RevenueCat, Apple, Google) may process data in their respective server regions, including the United States and the European Union, under their own privacy frameworks.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
